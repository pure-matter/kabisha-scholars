About

Kabisha Foundation

Talent should never be limited by financial circumstances. That belief is where everything started — and everything we do flows from it.

On this page

How it started Our mission Our partnership Long-term vision

Kabisha Foundation was created from a simple belief: talented students should not be limited by financial circumstances.

Growing up in The Gambia and later moving to the United States, I experienced firsthand how education can open doors and create opportunities that would otherwise seem out of reach. Many of the opportunities that shaped my own journey came from people and programs that invested in students and believed in their potential.

The Kabisha Foundation is my way of paying that forward.

Today, the Foundation supports Gambian students through scholarships, mentorship, and educational opportunities designed to help young people reach their goals and contribute to their communities. We place special emphasis on students from rural and underserved areas, where financial barriers often prevent talented students from continuing their education.

Our scholarship programs support students from secondary school through university helping them stay in school, complete their studies, and prepare for meaningful careers. When a student succeeds, their family, their community, and their country all benefit.

Our Partnership

Affiliated with JVH Museum

The Foundation is currently funded through personal contributions and operates in partnership with the Juffureh Heritage Museum — reflecting a shared commitment to preserving opportunity, knowledge, and community development in The Gambia.

Juffureh Heritage Museum

The JVH Museum is dedicated to preserving the history, culture, and heritage of Juffureh and The Gambia. Our partnership reflects a shared belief that investing in knowledge — past and future — is how communities grow and endure.
