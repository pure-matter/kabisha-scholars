University Scholarship

Kabisha Scholars
Program

Full-attendance university scholarship for Gambian students studying at home. Apply when you're ready — decisions are made on a rolling basis.

Apply Now Check Eligibility
Scholarship Benefits

What the Scholarship Covers

Full attendance at an accredited Gambian university or college — current and future enrollment only.

Tuition & Fees

Full academic year tuition and institutional fees at your enrolled institution.

Eligible Institutions

University of The Gambia (UTG), Gambia College, and other accredited Gambian colleges.

Renewable

Renewed each year subject to continued enrollment and academic performance.

Optional Extras

Tech mentorship and internship opportunities available to eligible students at no cost.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Selection

Meet the basic requirements to be eligible. Selection from eligible applicants weighs the priority criteria below.

Basic Requirements

  • Grade 12 graduate (WASSCE holder) or completed/ongoing diploma
  • Planning to study — or currently enrolled — at a Gambian institution
  • Admission letter or clear intention to enroll
  • Gambian citizen or resident
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Clear academic direction and career objective
  • No Funding for studying abroad
  • No Payment of arrears or old fees
  • No Course changes without reapplying
  • Yes Rolling application — apply anytime
  • Yes All applicants notified before the academic year

Priority Selection

From eligible applicants, these factors boost your chances significantly:

Rural background or school Science or technical field Engineering or construction Teaching career interest Willing to study rurally First-generation student

Application Process

How to Apply

No fixed deadline — rolling application. Apply early for best consideration.

01

Gather Documents

WASSCE results, ID, admission letter (if available), passport photo.

02

Complete the Form

Fill every section carefully. Use proper spelling — award letters use your data.

03

Write Your Essay

300–500 words on your goals, your major, and why a scholarship matters to you.

04

Submit & Wait

All applicants notified on or before the beginning of the academic year.
