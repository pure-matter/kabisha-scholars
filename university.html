Scholarship Benefits

What the Scholarship Covers

Full attendance at an accredited Gambian university or college. Here's what that includes.

📚

Tuition & Fees

Full academic year tuition and institutional fees at your enrolled institution.

🏫

Eligible Institutions

University of The Gambia (UTG), Gambia College, and other accredited Gambian colleges.

🔄

Renewable

Renewable each year subject to continued enrollment and academic performance.

💻

Optional Extras

Tech mentorship (CS & cybersecurity) and co-op opportunities for eligible students.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Selection

Meet the basic requirements to be eligible. Selection from eligible applicants is based on the priority criteria below.

Basic Requirements

  • Grade 12 graduate (WASSCE holder) or completed/ongoing diploma
  • Planning to study — or currently enrolled — at a Gambian institution
  • Admission letter or clear intention to enroll
  • Gambian citizen or resident
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Clear academic direction and career objective
  • ❌ No funding for studying abroad
  • ❌ Does not cover arrears or old fees
  • ❌ Changing your course requires reapplying
  • ✅ Rolling application — apply anytime
  • ✅ All applicants notified before the academic year

Priority Selection

From eligible applicants, these factors boost your chances significantly:

🏘️ Rural background or school 🔬 Science or technical field 🏗️ Engineering or construction 🏫 Teaching career interest 🌾 Willing to study rurally 💡 First-generation student
