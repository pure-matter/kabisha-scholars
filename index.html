Our Mission

Building Gambia's Future
Through Education

The Kabisha Scholarship Programs prioritize students with financial need, clear ambition, and a commitment to contributing to national development, especially those from rural and underserved communities.

A serious student with a clear direction and a commitment to Gambia's future.

Need-Based

Financial need is always weighted. Support reaches those who need it most.

Gambia-First

We fund Gambian institutions only — building talent that stays and contributes at home.

Rural Priority

Students from rural backgrounds and schools receive the highest priority.

Development Fields

Science, engineering, education, and technical fields are given priority in selection.
