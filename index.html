Kabisha Future Scholars Program

School fees covered plus a monthly stipend for students currently enrolled in high school. Designed for students who need financial support to stay in school — with strong priority for rural communities.

  • Full school fees covered
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Rural students — first priority
  • Optional tech mentorship

Kabisha Scholars Program

Full-attendance university scholarship for Gambian students pursuing higher education at accredited local institutions. A rolling application — apply when you're ready.

  • Full attendance coverage
  • UTG, Gambia College & more
  • Rolling application, no fixed deadline
  • Optional mentorship & co-op

Our Mission

Building Gambia's Future
Through Education

The Kabisha Scholarship Programs believe that every talented Gambian student deserves the chance to learn, grow, and give back. We prioritize students with financial need, clear ambition, and a commitment to contributing to national development — especially those from rural and underserved communities.

A serious student with a clear direction and a commitment to Gambia's future — that's who we're looking for.

Need-Based

Financial need is always weighted. Support reaches those who need it most.

Gambia-First

We fund Gambian institutions only — building talent that stays and contributes at home.

Rural Priority

Students from rural backgrounds and schools receive the highest priority consideration.

Development Fields

Science, engineering, education, and technical fields are given priority in selection.
